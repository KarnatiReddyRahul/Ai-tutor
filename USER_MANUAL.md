# AI Reverse Tutor — User Manual

## Overview

AI Reverse Tutor is an adaptive learning assessment platform. The AI interviews you on a topic instead of just answering your questions — following the Feynman learning principle.

## Getting Started

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Select a topic (e.g., REST APIs, Python, etc.)
3. The AI will ask you questions starting easy and adapting based on your answers
4. After the session, view your Skill X-Ray dashboard
5. Follow the personalized learning roadmap to improve weak areas

## Features

- **Adaptive Interviews**: Question difficulty adjusts to your responses
- **Knowledge Gap Detection**: Identifies strong/weak areas and misconceptions
- **Skill Dashboard**: Visual breakdown of skill levels per topic
- **Learning Roadmap**: Week-by-week plan to address weak areas
- **Multi-Language**: English, Telugu, Hindi
- **Assessment History**: Track your progress over time

## AI Providers

- Local: Ollama (Llama 3, Gemma, Mistral)
- Cloud: Google Gemini, Groq (bring your own API key)

## Tips

- Answer honestly for accurate gap detection
- Complete multiple assessments to track improvement
- Use the roadmap to guide your study sessions
